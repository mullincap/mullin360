Jim Mullin

President

With an early foundation in automotive retailing, Jim has deep experience in high-impact automotive real estate developments. He has been involved in over 1,200 automotive retail locations and 40+ developments across the US, Saudi Arabia, Brazil, and China.

Jacqueline Swanson

Mark Simmons
