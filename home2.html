Our Story & Vision

Rooted in Family.
Built for Legacy.

Mullin360 began as a family-owned firm founded by father-and-son team Art and Jim Mullin. For over three decades, we have served as owner, principal, investor, and development partner in high-quality real estate projects.

We are committed to impacting communities through innovative development, dedicated people, and loyal relationships. Our approach is strategic rather than transactional — we focus on creating properties that deliver positive, long-term contributions to their communities while maintaining the highest standards of service and quality.

As a modest, locally rooted company, we take our responsibility to clients, communities, and stakeholders seriously. Our proven stability and financial strength allow us to raise the bar for the industry.
