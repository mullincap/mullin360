“Committed to impacting communities through innovative development, dedicated people, and loyal relationships.”

Mullin Development was founded by father-son team Art and Jim Mullin. Over three decades, we’ve grown the business to shape automotive and commercial real estate at the local and national level.

Strategic rather than transactional — we invest in relationships as much as real estate, ensuring every project makes a long-term contribution to its community.
